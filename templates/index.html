About PomoCare

PomoCare is an advanced AI-powered fruit health monitoring system designed to help farmers and agricultural professionals detect diseases in pomegranate fruits with precision and speed.

Our deep learning model analyzes fruit images to identify common diseases including Alternaria, Anthracnose, Bacterial Blight, and Cercospora, providing instant diagnostic results.

The system offers detailed disease information, management strategies, and prevention techniques to help maintain healthy crops and maximize yield quality.

With real-time analysis and comprehensive reporting, PomoCare empowers farmers to make informed decisions and take timely action to protect their harvest.
